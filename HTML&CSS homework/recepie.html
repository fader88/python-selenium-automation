French Toast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Butter for cooking
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  2. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat and add butter.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, coating both sides.
  4. Cook each slice until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with maple syrup.
